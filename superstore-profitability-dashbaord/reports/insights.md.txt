# Superstore Profitability Insights

## 1. Discount Impact
- Orders discounted above 20% generate negative profit overall.
- Low and moderate discounts maintain strong margins.

**Action:** Introduce discount approval thresholds and limit high-discount campaigns.

---

## 2. Category Performance
- Technology and Office Supplies are consistently profitable.
- Furniture shows extremely low margins and several loss-making sub-categories.

**Action:** Reprice Furniture products and evaluate SKU-level performance.

---

## 3. Sub-Category Loss Drivers
Top loss contributors:
- Tables
- Bookcases
- Supplies

**Action:** Reduce discounting, renegotiate supplier costs, or discontinue underperformers.

---

## 4. Geographic Analysis
Losses are concentrated in a small number of states:
- Texas
- Ohio
- Pennsylvania
- Illinois

**Action:** Review regional discounting behavior and product mix.
